# Age Groups

Given the age of a person as an input, output their age group.

Here are the age groups you need to handle: </br>
**Child**: 0 to 11 </br>
**Teen**: 12 to 17 </br>
**Adult**: 18 to 64 </br>

**Sample Input** </br>
42

**Sample Output** </br>
Adult

> You can use the Boolean and operator to combine conditions, like x>0 and x<20
